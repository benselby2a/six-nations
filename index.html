App Help

            

                
My Predictions: Enter your score predictions for each fixture, choose your jokers, and set your tournament tries prediction. Predictions can only be entered/edited while predictions are unlocked.

                
Match Summary: View the live table, points breakdowns, tries tracking, and prize projections as results are entered.

                
Admin Functions: Fixture setup and score input, prediction editing, competitor management, prize fund setup, and recovery are available to admins only. Admins can lock predictions (typically once the tournament starts).
